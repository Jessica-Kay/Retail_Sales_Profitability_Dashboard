🧭 6. KPI Definitions
| KPI | Definition | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Total Sales** | Sum of all sales revenue | Measures overall business performance |
| **Total Profit** | Sum of all profit values | Shows financial health |
| **Profit Margin %** | Profit ÷ Sales | Indicates efficiency and pricing strength |
| **Top Categories** | Categories with highest sales/profit | Helps identify best‑performing product lines |
| **Return Rate** | % of orders returned | Highlights product or fulfillment issues |
| **Sales by Region** | Sales grouped by geographic region | Shows geographic performance trends |
